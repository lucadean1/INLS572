Project Idea: Starter Plant Guide

Purpose

This site helps first-time plant owners start confidently. It includes several starter pack recommendations depending on internal environments and personal priorities, a first-aid section for folks with a sick or struggling plant, and a set of extra resources such as recommended products/methods for those wanting to get into the weeds.

Target Audience

  • Busy students and first-time plant owners - Need a quick and easy guide for livening up their compact or inconvenient space without needing to dedicate all of their time to plant care.
  • Budget-conscious beginners - Looking for an affordable entry to plant ownership
  • Pet owners - Looking for clear recommendations for non-toxic plant setups that are safe around their furry friends

Features

Starter Packs

Three or more curated sets of beginner plants with differing light, care, and wallet requirements.

Example: Bright window, low maintenance - Aloe, Jade, Christmas Cactus

Plant Care

Some best practices and troubleshooting guide for the recommended species.

Example: Pothos, Yellowing Leaves - Overwatering / Low Light

Resources

A short list of affordable, must-have tools and products for new plant owners.

Need More Help?

For stubborn problems, users can reach out with questions through a simple form.
